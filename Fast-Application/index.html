Instructions

1. Complete one (1) FAST application form per person.
2. Complete the address and employment fields for the last five (5) years in full, leaving NO gaps.
3. At the end of the form you will be directed to our payment provider to pay the processing service fee - we cannot process your form unless you pay this fee.
4. When we receive your completed form and service fee, you will receive email confirmation from us within two business days.

FAST Application Service

1. On receipt of your application and our service fee payment, we will thoroughly check your data for errors and missing information.
2. We pay the government fee for the background and security checks using the card listed on your application.
3. Once your application is submitted, you will receive an email with your Traveler Profile and the next steps. Please check your junk mail if you cannot find an email from us. If you have not received an email from info@fastpassapplication.com within two business days please get in touch.
4. We guide you through the mandatory interview process.
5. We answer any questions you have relating to the FAST application process.

FAST Application Fees & Processing Times

1. USD $139.99 service fee for checking and processing your application. This fee is payable when you submit your form.
2. USD $50.00 for government background and security checks. This fee will be taken from the credit card listed on the form.

The average official processing time is 6 months. (Additional delays may result after government assessment.)

We aim to submit your application within 24 hours, although missing information may delay this.

FAST Refunds & Cancellation

1. The government fee (for background & security checks) is paid directly to the government and is non refundable.
2. We issue refunds under certain circumstances. If you believe that you should be refunded the IVT service fee, please e-mail info@fastpassapplication.com with the subject line: Name of Applicant: Refund
